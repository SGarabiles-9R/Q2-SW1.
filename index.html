Mobile Legends Characters

Miya, the Moonlight Archer Miya is the leader of the Moon Elf tribe and a skilled archer. Her tribe was once a peaceful, hidden race, but when war broke out with the Dark Lord, Miya was forced to fight to protect her people, relying on her sharp aim and mastery of archery to guide her through the battles. Miya unleashes a rain of arrows on all nearby enemies, hitting them multiple times in quick succession. The longer the fight continues, the more damage her arrows will do, and she becomes faster with each shot.
Irithel, the Jungle Heart Irithel is a skilled and courageous hunter who was born and raised in the wilds. With her companion, a giant panther named Leo, she seeks to protect her homeland from threats, using her formidable hunting skills and mastery of nature to keep the balance in the forest. Irithel unleashes a barrage of arrows in a wide area in front of her, dealing damage to all enemies in her path. She becomes immune to crowd control effects (like stuns or slows) while using this ultimate.
Clint, the West Justice Clint is a sharpshooter from the Wild West who grew up in a small town plagued by bandits. His father, a sheriff, was killed in a confrontation with outlaws, and Clint now roams the world seeking justice, using his exceptional marksmanship to protect the innocent. Clint fires multiple shots in a straight line, dealing damage to enemies hit. The more enemies he hits, the more powerful his ultimate becomes, and it can cause enemies to be stunned if they are hit by the center shot.
Hanabi, the Scarlet Flower Hanabi is the heir of the powerful Wuju Clan, known for their mastery of the "Irezumi" (tattoo magic). After a tragic betrayal led to the near destruction of her clan, she vowed revenge and protection for her remaining kin, wielding her powerful chakra-infused weapons to exact her wrath Hanabi throws a special flower that traps enemies in a line, dealing damage and reducing their movement speed. She can also heal herself and her allies when hitting enemies, making her harder to kill in battle.
Fanny, the Blade Dancer Fanny was once a cheerful and innocent girl who admired her father, an elite fighter. After her father's death at the hands of a betrayal, she became a ruthless and fast-paced assassin, seeking vengeance while using her agility and grappling skills to navigate the skies. Fanny Leaps at a enemy and each stack of Prey Mark (gained each skill she deploys her wires) increases this damage by 20%
